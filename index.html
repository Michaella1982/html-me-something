Ten Desert Island Records

I like to think everyone has desert island lists. You know- if you were trapped on a desert island and could only listen to ______, what would be on your list? These are just ten of many, many, MANY albums I think are worth checking out. This list is comprised of records that I've listened to time and time again and never gotten tired of.

  • Here Comes the Warm Jets - Brian Eno - 1974

  • Stankonia - Outkast - 2000

  • Minimal Wave Tapes Vol. 1 - Various Artists - 2010

  • Upstairs at Eric's - Yaz(oo) - 1982

  • Holiday - Magnetic Fields - 1993

  • Getz Au Go Go - (live album) the New Stan Getz Quartet feat. Astrud Gilberto - 1964

  • ChangesBowie - David Bowie - 1990

  • Which Way You Goin, Billy? - the Poppy Family feat. Susan Jacks - 1970

  • The Whole Story - Kate Bush - 1986

  • Dare - The Human League - 1982

I hope you enjoyed reading this, because I absolutely enjoyed making it.
